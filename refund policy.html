Refund Policy

Introduction

At Rhyno EV, we want to ensure that our customers are completely satisfied with their purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we offer a refund policy that is fair and easy to understand.

Eligibility for Refund

  • The item must be returned within 30 days of delivery.
  • The item must be in its original condition with all original packaging and accessories included.
  • The item must not have been used or damaged in any way.

Refund Process

  1. Contact our customer service team at info@rhyno.in to obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.
  2. Package the item securely and include all original packaging and accessories.
  3. Ship the item back to us at the following address: Rhyno EV, 123 Main St, Anytown, USA 12345.
  4. Once we receive the item, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Refund Amount

The refund amount will be the full purchase price of the item, minus any shipping costs.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item, please follow the same process as above. We will exchange the item for a new one of equal value.

Warranty

All Rhyno EV products come with a 1-year warranty. If your product is defective or malfunctions during this time, we will repair or replace it free of charge.
